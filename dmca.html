📋 About DMCA

lusthub.my.id respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users of this site to do the same. We respond to notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been copied in a way that constitutes copyright infringement and is accessible through this site, please follow the procedure below.

📝 DMCA Filing Procedure

To file a DMCA notice, please include the following information in your email:

1

Identify the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.

2

URL or link to the material on our site that you claim infringes your copyright.

3

Your contact information including full name, email address, and phone number.

4

A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

5

An electronic signature of the person authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

📧 Submit DMCA Request

Send your DMCA notice to:

📧 dmca@lusthub.my.id

We will respond to DMCA requests within 3-5 business days.

⚡ Processing Procedure

  • Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will promptly review the request.
  • If the request is deemed valid, the infringing content will be removed within 3-5 business days.
  • The notice sender will be notified after the content has been removed.
  • Repeat violations may result in permanent blocking of related content.

⚠️ Warning

False or unfounded DMCA notices may result in legal consequences. Make sure you are the rightful copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner before filing a claim.
