Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 30, 2026

📋 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how kumpulenak ("we", "us", or "this site") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you visit our website at kumpulenak.web.id.

By accessing and using this site, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📊 Information We Collect

1. Automatic Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP Address — for analytics and security
  • Browser type and version — for display compatibility
  • Operating system — for performance optimization
  • Pages visited — for usage analytics
  • Date and time of access — for server logs
  • Referrer URL — to understand traffic sources

2. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Theme preferences — storing your dark/light mode choice (kumpulenak_theme)
  • Browsing sessions — maintaining navigation state
  • Advertising — our advertising partners (Monetag, Adsterra) use cookies to display relevant ads

3. Information You Provide

If you contact us via email or contact form, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The content of your message

🔒 How We Use Your Data

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our website services
  • Analyze usage and trends to improve the site
  • Display relevant ads through our advertising partners
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

🤝 Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we work with the following third parties who may collect data through their own cookies:

  • Monetag — advertising network for monetization
  • Adsterra — banner advertising network
  • Vercel — hosting and CDN
  • Eporner API — video content provider

Each of these third parties has their own privacy policy governing data usage.

🍪 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect site functionality (e.g., theme preferences will not be saved).

👶 Child Protection

This site contains adult content and is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately.

🔄 Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
